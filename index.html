Insights

For Freshers - BTech 1st Year Students

Starting your BTech journey? Here are some insights to help you succeed in your first year:

  • Attend orientation programs to get familiar with your college and course.
  • Stay organized with a planner or digital calendar for assignments and deadlines.
  • Join study groups to collaborate with classmates and enhance your learning.
  • Visit professors during office hours if you need academic assistance.
  • Participate in extracurricular activities to develop new skills and make friends.
